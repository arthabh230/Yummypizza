WE NEED FOLLOWING INGREDIENTS

Dough

  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110¬∞F/45¬∞C)
  • 3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar

Sauce

  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp sugar

Toppings

  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives

INSTRUCTIONS

Preparing the Dough

In a large bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until frothy. Add the olive oil, salt, and 3 1/2 cups of flour. Mix until the dough comes together. Turn the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 10 minutes, adding more flour as needed, until smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

Making the Sauce

In a medium saucepan, combine the tomato sauce and tomato paste. Stir in the oregano, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, and sugar. Bring to a simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 20 minutes, or until thickened. Set aside to cool.

Assembling the Pizza

Preheat your oven to 475¬∞F (245¬∞C). Punch down the risen dough and turn it onto a lightly floured surface. Divide the dough in half and roll each half into a 12-inch circle. Transfer the dough to a lightly greased pizza pan or baking sheet. Spread the sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the sauce. Add your desired toppings, such as pepperoni, bell peppers, mushrooms, and olives. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.

Serving

Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve hot and enjoy the delicious cheesy goodness!
